          
     To see your project in your android device




              1) open the project on visual studio
              2) open the terminal and type 'npm install' and then 
                  'npm start'
              3) Install 'Expo go' app from play store on your android device
              4) open the 'Expo go' app and scan the QR code from editor terminal
              5) Then you can see your project on your device
